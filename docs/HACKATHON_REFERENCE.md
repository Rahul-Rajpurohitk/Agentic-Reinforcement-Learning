## Team Rules

- Teams of 1-3 members
- All team members must register individually
- Team Leader forms the team by entering teammates' registered email IDs before Round 1
- Once confirmed, teams cannot be changed — assignments are permanent
- Cross-campus and cross-company teams fully allowed
- Solo participants who advance to the Final Round will be matched/paired with other qualifying solo participants

---

## Eligibility

- College students pursuing any tech or relevant program across India
- Early-career tech professionals
- AI enthusiasts from anywhere in India
- No prior experience in Reinforcement Learning required

---

## Disqualification

- Plagiarism or copying code from external sources
- Submitting incomplete, invalid, or non-functional projects
- Not following OpenEnv standards or submission requirements
- Misconduct or disruptive behaviour during the hackathon
